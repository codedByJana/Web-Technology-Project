Insider Tips

The Art of Haggling

It is expected! Start by offering half the initial price and meet somewhere in the middle with a smile.

Navigation

Getting lost is part of the charm. Keep Al-Hussein Mosque as your landmark to easily find your way back to the main square.

Cash is King

While larger metal and carpet shops accept cards, bring Egyptian Pounds for spices, teas, and small souvenirs.
