Master the Ancient Crafts

Step out of the bustling alleys and into the studios of our master artisans. Whether you are shaping the clay of the Nile or forging intricate brass, immerse yourself in a hands-on journey through Egypt's timeless heritage.

The Clay of the Nile

Master the ancient art of Egyptian pottery. Feel the earth beneath your hands as our master artisans guide you through shaping, firing, and glazing your own traditional vessel.

Brass & Copper Art

Discover the rhythmic tap of the hammer. Learn to etch intricate geometric patterns into glowing brass, walking away with a custom lantern or plate.

Threads of the Bazaar

Sit at a traditional loom and weave vibrant cottons and silks. Understand the mathematics and patience behind Egypt's world-renowned textiles.
